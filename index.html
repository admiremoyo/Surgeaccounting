How Surge Accounting Can Help

Company Registrations

We assist entrepreneurs in navigating the complexities of starting a new business by providing comprehensive company registration services. From selecting the right business structure to submitting all required documentation, we ensure your company is legally established and compliant with all relevant regulations.

Tax Clearnace Renewals

Stay in good standing with the tax authorities by renewing your tax clearance certificates with our hassle-free service. We handle all the paperwork and submissions, ensuring you meet the renewal deadlines and remain compliant.

Tax Registrations

Whether you’re an individual or a business, our tax registration service makes it easy to get registered for the appropriate taxes. We guide you through the process, ensuring you meet all legal requirements and are properly registered with the tax authorities.

ZIMRA Returns & Compliance

Let us handle your ZIMRA returns, ensuring accurate and timely submissions. Our service covers VAT, PAYE, income tax, and more, keeping your business compliant with all Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) regulations.

NSSA Registrations and Renewal Certificates

We provide NSSA registration services for businesses, helping you comply with the National Social Security Authority (NSSA) requirements. We also manage the renewal of NSSA certificates to ensure your employees are covered under the national social security scheme.

PRAZ Registrations

If your business engages in public procurement, PRAZ registration is essential. We offer a smooth and efficient registration process with the Procurement Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (PRAZ), ensuring you meet all regulatory requirements.

Shop and Business Licenses

Operating a shop or business requires the right licenses. We assist in obtaining and renewing shop and business licenses, ensuring you adhere to all local regulations and can operate without any legal hurdles.

Bank Accounts Opening

Opening a bank account for your business or personal use can be daunting. We simplify the process by helping you prepare the necessary documentation and guiding you through the account opening process with various banks.

Accounting FAQ

 What accounting services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of accounting services, including bookkeeping, tax preparation and filing, financial statement preparation, payroll management, auditing, and financial consulting.

Do you provide tax services for individuals and businesses?

Yes, we offer tax services for both individuals and businesses, including tax planning, preparation, and filing in compliance with the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) regulations.

How do you ensure compliance with Zimbabwean tax laws?

Our team stays updated on the latest tax laws and regulations in Zimbabwe. We ensure that all our accounting practices adhere strictly to ZIMRA guidelines to keep you compliant and avoid any penalties.

What are your fees for accounting services?

Our fees vary depending on the specific services required and the complexity of your needs. We offer a free initial consultation to assess your requirements and provide a tailored quote.

How do you handle confidentiality and data security?

We take data security and confidentiality very seriously. All client information is stored securely, and we implement strict access controls to ensure that your financial data is protected at all times.

How can I contact you for more information or to schedule a consultation?

You can reach us through our website's contact form, email, or phone. We also offer in-person consultations at our office by appointment. Please visit our Contact Us page for more details.
